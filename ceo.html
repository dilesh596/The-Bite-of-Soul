OUR C.E.O.

Dilesh Ratnagopal Zingare

Dilesh Zingare is the visionary behind The Bite of Soul, leading the restaurant with a rare combination of ambition, creativity, and refined taste. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, he brings a modern perspective shaped by his studies in Information Technology, with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. His passion for innovation and excellence influences the restaurant’s approach to hospitality, quality, and customer experience. With a strong belief in delivering more than just a meal, he has helped build a brand that reflects sophistication, warmth, and distinction. Under his leadership, The Bite of Soul continues to grow as a destination where premium dining and genuine hospitality meet. His vision ensures that every experience at the restaurant feels thoughtful, elevated, and truly memorable.
